About Solutions

Strategy And Design
As your Salesforce partner, Soft Innovas helps you in coming up with the right implementation strategy and software design. Especially if you are entering the world of cloud-based CRM for the first time, we guide you every step of the way in understanding the architecture of the platform and how it works. Rest assured that our Salesforce professionals would focus on getting you the highest possible returns on your investment. Our strategy and design services revolve around creating an ideal blueprint of your implementation plan and the CRM platform to be set up as per your requirements. Moreover, we suggest Salesforce best practices to you and recommend tools that are in sync with your business objectives. Right from need discovery and roadmap design to prototyping and technical delivery assistance, we help you chart out the best strategy and design for implementing the CRM platform.

Full-scale Implementation
If you are willing to go all the way, we help you with full-scale Salesforce implementation according to your needs and preferences. Our certified Salesforce experts assist you in planning, designing, developing, and delivering your Salesforce implementation project the way you envision it. Whether you are willing to implement a single Salesforce Cloud or more, you can rest assured that we would bring your project to fruition. As your Salesforce partner, our full-scale implementation services include everything from need assessment and technical design to legacy data migration and third-party integrations.

Salesforce Consultation
3 Years of experience in the industry have equipped our professionals with the tricks of the trade and nitty-gritty details about the CRM platform. Through our Salesforce consultation services, we provide personalized resolutions to your problems and get your queries answered at every stage of implementation. From suggesting suitable tools to helping you troubleshoot problems, you can rely on our Salesforce consultation services to have a smooth run!

Salesforce Integration
Salesforce is a customizable platform and can be readily integrated with a plethora of different applications and services. As your Salesforce implementation partners, we help you with third-party integrations to enhance the overall functionality of Salesforce. Depending on the objectives you want to achieve and the users you are targeting, we help you sync your Salesforce data and business logic with suitable platforms. This would prevent your team members from switching between different platforms by providing them with a centralized interface.
